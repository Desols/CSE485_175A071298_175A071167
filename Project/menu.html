Data, Tools, and Resources from Mekong Partners

The Mekong Water Data Platform (MWDP) is a collaborative effort of Mekong stakeholders and global partners to promote the sustainable management of the Mekong River basin and the wellbeing of its people. This page includes a growing catalogue of resources (e.g., data, models, analyses, and expert services) available from MWDP collaborators. This list is being updated regularly. We kindly ask for your contribution as well! Those who wish to link or share their resources with the Mekong community may do so.
